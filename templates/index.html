    Our Medical Departments

    JESP Hospital offers a wide range of specialized departments dedicated to providing expert care across every area of health. Our team of professionals ensures personalized treatment, modern technology, and compassionate support for every patient.

    • Cardiology
    • Neurology
    • Hepatology

    Cardiology

    At JESP Hospital, our Cardiology Department is dedicated to the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of heart and vascular conditions. With a team of experienced cardiologists, advanced diagnostic technology, and a patient-centered approach, we provide comprehensive care for everything from routine heart check-ups to complex cardiac emergencies.

    We offer services including ECG, echocardiography, stress testing, hypertension management, heart failure care, and interventional procedures. Whether you’re managing a chronic condition or experiencing new symptoms, our goal is to ensure your heart health is in expert hands.o

    Neurology

    The Neurology Department at JESP Hospital specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system. Our expert neurologists are equipped to manage a wide range of conditions including stroke, epilepsy, migraines, Parkinson’s disease, multiple sclerosis, and neuropathies.

    Using advanced neuroimaging and diagnostic tools, we provide accurate assessments and personalized treatment plans to improve each patient’s quality of life. With compassionate care and cutting-edge expertise, we’re here to support you through every step of your neurological health journey.

    Hepatology

    JESP Hospital’s Hepatology Department is dedicated to the diagnosis, treatment, and management of liver-related conditions. Our team of skilled hepatologists provides expert care for a wide range of liver diseases, including hepatitis, fatty liver disease, cirrhosis, and liver failure.

    We combine advanced diagnostics, non-invasive testing, and personalized treatment plans to ensure early detection and effective management. Whether you require routine liver check-ups or specialized long-term care, we are committed to delivering compassionate, comprehensive liver care

    Pediatrics

    At JESP Hospital, our Pediatrics Department provides specialized care for infants, children, and adolescents in a warm, family-friendly environment. Our pediatricians are dedicated to ensuring your child’s health and development through every stage of growth.

    From routine check-ups and vaccinations to the management of acute and chronic illnesses, we offer comprehensive pediatric services with a focus on safety, comfort, and compassion. We believe every child deserves a healthy start and a bright future — and we’re here to make that happen.

    Ophthalmologists

    JESP Hospital’s Ophthalmology Department provides expert care for all your eye health needs. Our skilled ophthalmologists specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of conditions affecting vision, including cataracts, glaucoma, macular degeneration, and diabetic retinopathy.

    We offer state-of-the-art diagnostic tools and surgical interventions, from routine eye exams to advanced procedures. Whether you need corrective lenses, eye surgery, or ongoing management of a chronic eye condition, our team is committed to helping you see the world clearly and comfortably.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Got questions? Here are some common queries we get from our patients.

    What are your clinic's operating hours?

    We are open Monday to Saturday, from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

    Do I need an appointment to see a doctor?

    Appointments are preferred to minimize waiting times, but we do accept walk-ins based on availability.

    Do you accept private insurance?

    Yes, we accept most major private health insurance providers. Please bring your insurance card when you visit.

    Can I get a prescription refill online?

    Yes, registered patients can request prescription refills through our patient portal or by calling reception.

    What should I bring for my first appointment?

    Please bring a valid photo ID, your insurance details (if applicable), and any medical records relevant to your visit.

    How do I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

    You can call us directly or use the online portal at least 24 hours before your appointment to cancel or reschedule.
